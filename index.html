Our Services

Innovation, empowerment, community impact – transforming lives through purposeful services.

The wide range of services provided by MB Foundation demonstrates our dedication to both innovation and societal impact. Our initiatives cover multiple tech areas, from pioneering new programming languages like PyConda to supporting the next generation of programmers through free education with Code Explorer and cutting-edge AGI research with GalactoMind AGI. We provide high-quality software solutions through our software company, MB WEBBER'S, connect developers worldwide through Dev Line Community, and enlighten the community through News World. Our all-encompassing strategy guarantees a constructive and revolutionary impact on education, technology, and community development.

MB Foundation is committed to innovation and societal impact. From AGI research to free programming education, we pioneer new languages, deliver news, build communities, and provide top-notch software solutions.

Our Team

Meet Our Experienced Team Members

MB Foundation thrives under the visionary leadership of Pabitra Banerjee, who serves as both the Founder & CEO of the foundation itself and the software development company, MB WEBBER'S. Pabitra's profound passion for astrophysics and mathematics, coupled with his commitment to bridging the gap between technology and everyday life, sets the foundation's core ethos.

Subham Narayan Deb assumes the role of CEO at Code Explorer, an integral part of MB Foundation's educational initiatives. Subham's technological expertise ensures the delivery of cutting-edge solutions, aligning with the foundation's mission of positive impact through education and innovation.

Anirban Mandal spearheads Dev Line Community as its CEO, contributing to the foundation's collaborative efforts in fostering a supportive global community of developers. Anirban's commitment to creating an inclusive space for developers aligns seamlessly with MB Foundation's broader goals.

Ahan Kumar Ghosh takes the helm as the CEO of News World, guiding the platform in delivering reliable and timely news. Ahan's leadership ensures that News World plays a crucial role in keeping the audience well-informed on global happenings, a key aspect of the foundation's commitment to transparency and information dissemination.

Debabrata Chatterjee leads two critical initiatives within MB Foundation, serving as the CEO of both PyConda Programming Language and GalactoMind AGI. His leadership in language development and AGI research underscores the foundation's dedication to transformative technologies and advancements in programming and artificial intelligence.

Together, this cohesive team embodies the essence of MB Foundation, working collaboratively to create a positive and transformative influence in technology, education, and community building. Their collective efforts drive the foundation's commitment to empowering dreams, fostering innovation, and transforming lives.
